PCOS / PCOD Awareness

What is PCOS? Polycystic Ovary Syndrome is a hormonal disorder causing enlarged ovaries with small cysts on the outer edges.

Causes: Hormonal imbalance, genetics, insulin resistance, obesity.

Prevention / Management: Healthy diet, regular exercise, maintaining healthy weight, stress management, and medical treatment if required.

Breast Cancer Awareness

What is Breast Cancer? Breast cancer occurs when cells in the breast grow uncontrollably, forming a malignant tumor.

Causes: Genetics (BRCA mutations), age, family history, hormonal factors, lifestyle factors like smoking and alcohol.

Prevention: Regular self-exams, mammograms, healthy lifestyle, limiting alcohol, avoiding smoking, and consulting a doctor for high-risk cases.
